Teaching

I have taught semantics, pragmatics, and experimental linguistics at various levels and across institutions in Asia and North America (see the list). I designed the semantics curriculum for a undergraduate linguistics program, which included both an introductory and an advanced course in formal semantics, as well as for a MA (coursework) program.

The instituations where I have taught:

  • Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (NTU) 2024 - present
  • University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC) 2022 - 2023
  • Guangdong University of Foreign Studies (GDUFS) 2020 - 2022

On this page, I share my course syllabi and/or course materials, which I hope you'll find helpful for designing a semantics course or studying semantics.
