More specifically, I have worked on Mandarin (and its dialects), Cantonese, English, and Japanese in the following areas:

  • Form--Meaing Mismatches

  • Interrogative Meaning

  • Dynamic Meaning

  • Focus--Interrogative Interaction

  • Quantification and Measurement

  • Ellipsis
