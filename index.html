Haoze Li | 李昊澤

I'm now an assistant professor at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore. My research interest lies primarily in semantics and pragmatics, as well as their interactions with syntax. I combine theoretical and experimental methods to address fundamental questions about form and meaning in natural languages.

I received a B.A. from Hunan University (Changsha, China), a M.Phil from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, and a Ph.D. from New York University. I subsequently obtained the postdoctoral quanification of Associate Professor in Linguistics ("副高级职称" in Chinese), a rank comparable to the German "Habilitation".
